--- layout: post title: "Family Dance to support Dereel's Men's Shed" author: Scott Weston --- The Dereel and Surrounding Communities (D.A.S.C.) Men's Shed Inc. are holding a family 60/40 dance in the Dereel Hall on the 2nd of October 2010 between 7.30pm and 11.30pm. Everyone is welcome.

There will be a live band (Steve & Alan, "The two of us") and entry will be by gold coin donation. BYO food and drink (tea and coffee will be provided).

There will also be donated goods for sale with proceeds going to the men's shed, come and show your support!

For more information about the dance or about the men's shed please contact A. Shirreff on 5346-1557
