--- layout: post title: "Dereel NBN Tower delayed 6 months" author: Scott Weston --- Even though our council approved the tower it appears at least one resident in our community (I can't imagine who) has lodged a complaint to VCAT. This means our tower will now be delayed a further 6 months at least. It's a sad day for those of us who need a broadband solution ASAP but it's just how our planning system works. As mentioned in previous posts it is extremely unlikely that VCAT will rule against our councils decision, it's just a shame that the wheels turn so slowly and we have to wait so long for a decision. When I get the details of the hearing (date, time and location) I will post them here, VCAT has a public gallery and anyone can attend their hearings.
