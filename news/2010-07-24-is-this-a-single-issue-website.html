--- layout: post title: "Is this a single issue website?" author: Scott Weston --- As Dale rightly pointed out, it seems like this site is actually only about getting Dereel a mobile tower. While this issue is what moved me to create this website it is my hope that enough people in the community will get involved that it will cover a lot more. This of course would require people in the community to write posts and submit content and nothing would make me happier than to see this happen. So if you have an event, or an issue or just some information that you want to publish that you think is useful for the Dereel community (and yes I will even happily publish posts from people critical of Dereel getting a mobile tower, I'm not Stalin) then all you need to do is register for an account on this site then send me a message stating you'd like to write content and I'll enable your account to submit posts - I will have to moderate it before it's published (because I don't want to allow spam) but that's all there is to it. I look forward to seeing what we get.
