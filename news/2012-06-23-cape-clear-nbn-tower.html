--- layout: post title: "Cape Clear NBN Tower" author: Scott Weston --- The Cape Clear NBN communications tower was approved by the Golden Plains Shire on the same day as the Dereel tower. No residents of Cape Clear took the shire to VCAT to object and so construction was able to start. This is where the Dereel tower would have been up to if no-one had objected. The foundations are poured and the tower has been delivered to site.
