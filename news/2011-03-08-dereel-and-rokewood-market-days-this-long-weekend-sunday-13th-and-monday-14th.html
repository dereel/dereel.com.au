--- layout: post title: "Dereel and Rokewood market days this long weekend - Sunday 13th and Monday 14th" author: Scott Weston --- If you like a good market day you're in for a treat this weekend. Both Dereel and Rokewood are holding market days, Dereel on Sunday the 13th followed by Rokewood on Monday the 14th. Dereel's market day will be held in the Dereel community hall and Rokewood's will be held in the Rokewood RSL hall. A sausage sizzle with hamburgers and hot and cold drinks will be available. For further information or booking details please call (03)53461525 or (03)52388224.
