--- layout: post title: "Community Meeting about planned burning operations: Tuesday 3rd of August 2010" author: Scott Weston --- There will be a community meeting on Tuesday the 3rd of August in the Dereel Community Centre between 6:30pm and 8:30pm to discuss planned burning operations to be undertaken in and around Dereel, Enfield and Berringa.

You are invited to attend to listen and to ask any questions you may have about the operations. The draft plans can be viewed at www.dse.vic.gov.au/fire.

For more information you can contact Merydth Whitehead (DSE Land & Fire Planning Manager) on 5335-0729 or Lee Glesson (DSE Fuel Management Officer) on 5321-6888
