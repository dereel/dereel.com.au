--- layout: post title: "Golden Plains Shire seeking people to manage the Dereel Hall" author: Scott Weston --- The Golden Plains Shire is seeking expressions of interest from local residents who would like to help manage the hall and the community centre.

Expressions of interest should respond to the following criteria. Ability to:

  • represent the interests of the local community

  • promote access to and use of the hall and community centre

  • contribute to effective management of the facility


Expressions of interest forms can be requested on 5220-7111 or online at www.goldenplains.vic.gov.au. Completed forms should be addressed to:

Rod Nicholls
Chief Executive Officer
Golden Plains Shire Council
PO Box 111
Bannockburn
Victoria 3331

All forms must be received by 5pm on 15th of October 2010
